# Demographics Analytics

Demographics analytics provides creators with detailed insights into the composition of their world’s audience in Meta Horizon Worlds. Through the developer dashboard, creators can view aggregated data on visitor attributes such as country, age, gender, and primary language. This information helps creators better understand who is engaging with their world, enabling them to tailor content, optimize engagement strategies, and make informed decisions to grow and improve their experiences.

## How to use the Demographics Analytics dashboard

- Navigate to the [Meta Horizon Developer Dashboard](https://developers.meta.com/horizon/manage) and select your world.
- Select Insights to open the analytics dashboard.
- Select Demographics from the available options.
- Use the filters to select period, and date ranges.
- Review the charts and tables to understand your world’s audience.
- Export data as CSV for further analysis if needed.

## Demographics Metrics

| **Metric** | **Description** |
| --- | --- |
| Unique visitors by country | The % of unique users visiting the world based on their geographic location. |
| Unique new visitors by country | The % of unique users visiting the world for the first time based on their geographic location. |
| Unique visitors by gender | The % of unique users visiting the world based on their gender. |
| Unique new visitors by gender | The % of unique users visiting the world for the first time based on their gender. |
| Unique visitors by primary account language | The % of unique users visiting the world based on their primary account language. |
| Unique new visitors by primary account language | The % of unique users visiting the world for the first time based on their primary account language. |

**Note**: Demographic data is aggregated and estimated based on user-provided information.
