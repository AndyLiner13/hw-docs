## Copy an item listing link

Once you’ve created and listed an item or linkted the item to a quest or listed an in-world item through the editor, you can generate links for those items.

To copy a created item’s link, use the following process:

- Open your browser and navigate to the [Developer Dashboard’s Inventory](https://developers.meta.com/horizon/manage/org/inventory) or select **Avatar Items or In-World Items** from the left menu options.
