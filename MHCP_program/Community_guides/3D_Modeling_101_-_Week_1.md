# 3D Modeling 101 - Week 1

              Ready to transform your creative vision into 3D reality? Join MHCP Mentor
SpaceGlitterUnicorn for a comprehensive introduction to 3D modeling, designed
specifically to give you the confidence and skills to create your first 3D assets. • Get acquainted with the Blender interface
• Create your first Meta Horizon-ready model
