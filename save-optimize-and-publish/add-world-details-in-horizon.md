# Add world details

As a creator, you can add details to your world to help people discover it. These details include names, descriptions, photos, trailers, and more. In the Developer Dashboard, these details are referred to as *World Metadata*.

## Tips for adding world details

* Use a short, compelling name for your world.
* In the description, briefly let people know what they can do or experience in your world.
* Take a photo that captures specific parts of your world.

## To take a photo of your world:

- While wearing your headset in your world, go to **Preview Mode**.
- Open your **Personal Menu**, then select the Camera icon.
- Grab the selfie stick by holding down either grip.
- Select the reverse arrows to reverse the camera, then use the index trigger to take a photo.

## How to add World Metadata in the Developer Dashboard

- Open your browser and navigate to the [My worlds](https://developers.meta.com/horizon/manage/worlds/) on the Developer Dashboard.
- Select the world you want to add world metadata to.
- From the left-side navigation, select **Distribute** > **World Metadata**.

From here, you can find options for editing both the **Specs & Ratings** and the **Assets** for your world.

### Specs & Ratings

Here, you can set comfort ratings and content specs for your world. These help improve discoverability, accessibility, and platform compliancem through the following confirgurable options:

* Set the type of device your world is best playable on.
* Set the content rating for your world.
* Choose up to three genres for your world.
* Set whether your world is searchable in the Meta Horizon Store.

### Assets

Here you can upload images and media that represent your world. These assets help users preview and discover your experience. Note that all worlds assets must also meet our [asset design guidelines](https://developers.meta.com/horizon/resources/asset-guidelines) for apps on the Meta Horizon Store.

Here you can upload, replace, or delete the following kinds of assets:

* Cover art in landscape, square, or portrait orientations. All images should be 24-bit PNGs.
* Hero cover art, which is the main asset on your worldâ€™s WDP and will be used if your world is featured in the Store. It must be the same design as your cover art and requires a 10:3 aspect ratio with a 3000px x 900px resolution.
* Optional icon and logo. Your icon and/or logo must be the same design as your cover art and requires a 10:3 aspect ratio with a 3000px x 900px resolution.
* Optional trailers must to be between 30 seconds and 2 minutes in length. Any trailer requires a 16:9 aspect ratio with between a 1080p (min) 2k (max) resolution with an MP4/H.264/AAC format.
