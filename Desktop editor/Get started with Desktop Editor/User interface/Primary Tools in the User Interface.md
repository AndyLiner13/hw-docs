## Camera speed controls

![Camera speed icon](https://scontent-dfw5-3.xx.fbcdn.net/v/t39.2365-6/475752940_641654761705812_835031851319945568_n.png?_nc_cat=109&ccb=1-7&_nc_sid=e280be&_nc_ohc=6AQZNeC-OA8Q7kNvwGJfGvx&_nc_oc=AdlkwqXMHwm91VkVa903-JGEV_eMGwKgloJpbXCE8vke_jkxusOli1AQfGrqmM55F1M&_nc_zt=14&_nc_ht=scontent-dfw5-3.xx&_nc_gid=hajYgJMwzk17FroMzHoSiA&oh=00_AfjdwcUsndsCoUYh5MGm0pjK4_CrWH5sjvZXYfCkTrEifg&oe=692FCCDA)

The Camera speed controls enable you to adjust the camera speed within your world and adjust the location of the camera.

Camera speed is the rate at which the camera moves when you’re navigating through your world. With this, you can customize the navigation experience to suit your needs. For example, you might find a faster camera speed useful for quickly exploring large areas, or a slower camera speed more suitable for precise movements.

![Camera speed controls](https://scontent-dfw5-1.xx.fbcdn.net/v/t39.2365-6/475164398_641654801705808_806370826786711348_n.png?_nc_cat=110&ccb=1-7&_nc_sid=e280be&_nc_ohc=2lIv-zkeDbYQ7kNvwGrvdyQ&_nc_oc=AdnzJe2c3GBhyhyCvXcg8nl3K1cJ7sVbWihDrfAuOs2w7_OGCiupguROeshGx1QENYk&_nc_zt=14&_nc_ht=scontent-dfw5-1.xx&_nc_gid=hajYgJMwzk17FroMzHoSiA&oh=00_AfhZfsGROpMaYeIryc3BuaA60iMPpWNHaNd1w8Dl5utM3w&oe=692F979C)

You can ajust the location of the camera by right-clicking the camera icon and using the arrow keys or WASD keys to move the camera around.

## World simulation toggle

![World simulation toggle](https://scontent-dfw5-1.xx.fbcdn.net/v/t39.2365-6/496303400_715823364288951_3074126862394797930_n.png?_nc_cat=111&ccb=1-7&_nc_sid=e280be&_nc_ohc=t9LrfDxiH7EQ7kNvwHp8XfO&_nc_oc=Adm34aybAdYaUEtceyvPFIxcD-9rEbduRXJeenC00FJ2fNJDxuNWItlICVUKPI45J9s&_nc_zt=14&_nc_ht=scontent-dfw5-1.xx&_nc_gid=hajYgJMwzk17FroMzHoSiA&oh=00_AfhO97yQNrCZN6lZlLovAtBs34S2Ufnk5RSANEfsKqR9aA&oe=692FAE33)

The world simulation toggle allows you to turn off and on your world simulation; the toggle is set to **World Sim Off** by default.

Clicking the toggle will change the option to **World Sim On**, and will start your world simulation. This includes launching preStart() and start() methods of your scripts, which pre-populates the object pool.

Clicking the toggle again will turn world simulation off and revert the toggle back to **World Sim Off**.
