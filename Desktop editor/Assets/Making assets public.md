# Making assets public

This feature is only available to members of the [Meta Horizon Creator Program](https://developers.meta.com/horizon-worlds/programs). Learn more and join today.

## Creating a public asset

You can publish 3D models and asset templates to Public assets. This allows other creators to import and use your assets in their own worlds.
When other creators import and use your assets, they can make changes to the asset locally, but their changes will not affect the published version of the asset.

To make your asset public:

- Select any asset in **My Assets** by clicking on its preview.

  ![Asset's preview](https://scontent-dfw5-1.xx.fbcdn.net/v/t39.2365-6/497968432_719235307281090_6431432537667238546_n.png?_nc_cat=101&ccb=1-7&_nc_sid=e280be&_nc_ohc=axWmEqeEESQQ7kNvwF35-VG&_nc_oc=Admx82gAUgmjkdY6zvL7gAYGlpxXUU9PtDqJMQiVMUjxie3iPkYCCvOx34iemElNeCg&_nc_zt=14&_nc_ht=scontent-dfw5-1.xx&_nc_gid=MLLFvsec2oCNMbsuxwT6Ew&oh=00_AfhZ6pdTsW6SgiYNwSuzSlOnreITU1I8yyp5Onw0isCbRw&oe=692FC6E2)
- In the **Asset details** panel, click **Make asset public**.

  ![Asset details - make asset public button](https://scontent-dfw5-1.xx.fbcdn.net/v/t39.2365-6/497503303_719235290614425_6420250686078170815_n.png?_nc_cat=103&ccb=1-7&_nc_sid=e280be&_nc_ohc=ktTIkmmAy28Q7kNvwEiJSmw&_nc_oc=Adl3ZXw533ALehmSrtTgUBmEmnAesbRKnKU14aRfNrzdJ9cnJQn-Euy73PLRuhcAKdI&_nc_zt=14&_nc_ht=scontent-dfw5-1.xx&_nc_gid=MLLFvsec2oCNMbsuxwT6Ew&oh=00_AfhzGXcFho5d8iQTviykNgGGMEMM3FF1sMB3j9WlBd7k_g&oe=692FC46B)
- Check that the **name** and **description** will still be relevant once the asset is shared with other creators, and update it if needed.

  ![Make asset public modal window](https://scontent-dfw5-2.xx.fbcdn.net/v/t39.2365-6/498189666_719235273947760_896622341323617970_n.png?_nc_cat=107&ccb=1-7&_nc_sid=e280be&_nc_ohc=84zTk-XRFa0Q7kNvwFIX8Ge&_nc_oc=AdlKmBx06R7u4BB66Kz40WeqadnQ6ySV0YeVwjkwNTn_zYKMJGDrKn4hHNQdBw_6kso&_nc_zt=14&_nc_ht=scontent-dfw5-2.xx&_nc_gid=MLLFvsec2oCNMbsuxwT6Ew&oh=00_AfgwbEcDVeCRgaDvgS3oEaJPRHJ9_B7LE5gSJAUZPEySiA&oe=692FA97C)
- Add the relevant categories and tags to make it easier for creators to discover your asset.
- Upload a thumbnail to give creators an idea of what your asset looks like.
- Click **Make public**.

Once your asset is published, go to **Public assets** to view your published asset. You can use the search box to find it quickly by the name.

![Search published asset](https://scontent-dfw5-2.xx.fbcdn.net/v/t39.2365-6/496638209_719235303947757_3925284670688064436_n.png?_nc_cat=102&ccb=1-7&_nc_sid=e280be&_nc_ohc=lT1jRkbAxUYQ7kNvwE81oPC&_nc_oc=AdkFX7qtTG_-mv1N3i_EL7LzrTBb9riSgK3MaVkE2Jxq8-vN4okVTsHdWZgkwJe7q0E&_nc_zt=14&_nc_ht=scontent-dfw5-2.xx&_nc_gid=MLLFvsec2oCNMbsuxwT6Ew&oh=00_AfhOFX9UsZNH0Xcz4pFSWVEtd1uIEnPF031GoOams_R7Fw&oe=692FC5C0)

To view all the assets you have published, navigate to **My Assets** and click on **Added to public assets**.

![Added to public assets section](https://scontent-dfw5-2.xx.fbcdn.net/v/t39.2365-6/496813933_719235287281092_1117305794773549864_n.png?_nc_cat=107&ccb=1-7&_nc_sid=e280be&_nc_ohc=geY1uG5KQlYQ7kNvwHIAWNp&_nc_oc=AdlMdpzW-TWzDDBgy4YLhkSwXvu2MvR2phoFHbdGg7itndA-k9EtGAbwEo0uqDjn_uI&_nc_zt=14&_nc_ht=scontent-dfw5-2.xx&_nc_gid=MLLFvsec2oCNMbsuxwT6Ew&oh=00_AfjdhNq73eyPC4-E_bvacXihl4ZCt3BEqLpJEQXpM-7FlA&oe=692FC253)

## Updating a public asset

You can create a new version of your asset, and choose whether to make the changes available only to yourself or to other creators.

To make changes to your public asset:

- Find your asset in **My Assets**.
- Click on the asset preview to open the **Asset details** panel.
- Modify asset information as needed, including the name, description, category, tags, thumbnail, files and more.
- Click **Publish update** to make your changes available to other creators. If you don’t select **Publish update**, the changes will only be applied to your own version of the asset.

  ![Publish update button](https://scontent-dfw5-2.xx.fbcdn.net/v/t39.2365-6/497494535_719235300614424_4669335376753486320_n.png?_nc_cat=100&ccb=1-7&_nc_sid=e280be&_nc_ohc=klKzrF4kEc4Q7kNvwG1s7br&_nc_oc=Adm4IrwuDrHEA5t0p_GYnzydtDOZwvd9R8Opw3cMZ1cQiYCerKMcX47fxfr34Kc26Ws&_nc_zt=14&_nc_ht=scontent-dfw5-2.xx&_nc_gid=MLLFvsec2oCNMbsuxwT6Ew&oh=00_AfhXirMssa-4hfcK65ZYy7-kViXoqSG-4yPrXpqM8AFzZw&oe=692FC047)
- Click **Publish update** to add the updated version of your asset to **Public assets**.

  ![Publish update modal window](https://scontent-dfw5-2.xx.fbcdn.net/v/t39.2365-6/497808490_719235297281091_4406831414252185964_n.png?_nc_cat=106&ccb=1-7&_nc_sid=e280be&_nc_ohc=iV96XkcBs8AQ7kNvwH23crd&_nc_oc=Adk0vaHXiAi4FM6GfqteHdOWC-81ItjKTK6ihQrN_xwEJOq3norS6P8aSNDfIf_fQBw&_nc_zt=14&_nc_ht=scontent-dfw5-2.xx&_nc_gid=MLLFvsec2oCNMbsuxwT6Ew&oh=00_AfjWLzOJcumFu_QYbfXL1dwaAGJj2cBk5Klj2FUYB6EDOQ&oe=692FC8F1)

When changes become public, you will see a notification. Following the update, creators will be able to view only the latest version of the asset in **Public assets**. To get a new asset version, creators will need to visit **Public assets** and manually import the latest changes. Previously imported asset versions will not be updated.

## Managing different versions of your public asset

When you update your public asset files, a new version of the asset is created. To manage different versions of your asset open **Version history**. You can do it by clicking on **Version history** in the **Asset details** panel.

## Removing an asset from Public assets

To remove your asset from Public assets:

- Find your asset in **My Assets**.
- Click the asset preview to open the **Asset details** panel.
- Click on the icon next to **Publish update**.

  ![Remove from public assets button](https://scontent-dfw5-3.xx.fbcdn.net/v/t39.2365-6/497545783_719235280614426_317010417810783860_n.png?_nc_cat=108&ccb=1-7&_nc_sid=e280be&_nc_ohc=sSyCa1Yf0EIQ7kNvwGukxGf&_nc_oc=AdkBN5TWTcBBzGZX6mCZexkpi-6bE-XODXFZlnDetsDYvwTKFeqejged9vYTQ3veYWY&_nc_zt=14&_nc_ht=scontent-dfw5-3.xx&_nc_gid=MLLFvsec2oCNMbsuxwT6Ew&oh=00_Afhcq7db0pbVOKNsOU5Agu7zLjtGwK7Rr53VoI6BxdfQfQ&oe=692FC4A0)

**Note:** After removing the asset from public assets, it will no longer be discoverable by other creators. However, creators who have already imported this asset to their world will still be able to use it.
